PicoViz examples

Lightweight, self-contained molecular visualizations — ribbons, spheres, surfaces, and trajectories with no 3DMol.js and no CDN.

  • Menin + Revumenib — spectrum ribbon cartoon, ligand sticks, and a translucent molecular surface with a label. 177 KB (vs 594 KB with 3DMol.js)
  • Abl kinase DFG-flip — 126-frame trajectory, spectrum ribbon with PHE157 highlighted as sticks. 4.6 MB (vs 45.6 MB with 3DMol.js)
  • Abl kinase DFG-flip (spheres) — 126-frame trajectory, gray ribbon with PHE157 as green spheres. 4.6 MB (vs 45.6 MB with 3DMol.js)

Higher-detail builds (same data, more polygons, ~no size change): menin (ultra) · DFG-flip (high).

Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, right-drag to pan. Trajectories play automatically; use the slider to scrub. Generate your own with picoviz build input.xtc --top top.pdb -o out.html.
